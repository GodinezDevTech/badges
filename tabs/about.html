About Me_

Hi, I’m Steven Godinez, a first-generation college student from Houston (Go Astros!) pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Mathematics at Northeastern University, with plans to graduate in 2027. My passion for technology and problem-solving has driven me to explore the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, and I’m already preparing for a Masters of Science in Artificial Intelligence with a specialization in Machine Learning to deepen my expertise.

Currently, I’m building innovative projects like an AI-Driven Cyber Threat Detection and Response System, designed to predict and combat cyber threats in real-time. These projects reflect my commitment to creating impactful, cutting-edge solutions to real-world challenges.

Outside of academics, I strive to bring curiosity and creativity to my work, whether it’s organizing HackBeanpot’s hackathon or refining my skills through coding challenges. When I’m not immersed in tech, you might find me riding my Harley 2013 Fat Bob, trying out new coffee shops or powerlifting. Feel free to explore my GitHub, ask about my latest AI project, or connect with me to chat about tech, bikes, or how to tackle the next big challenge!
